# join-AIC — Onboarding & Community Hub for AIC

Welcome to **join-AIC** — an onboarding portal for anyone interested in joining, collaborating, or connecting to the AIC (Adaptive Intelligence Circle) ecosystem.

Goals of the repo:

- Brief explanation of philosophy & governance.

- Step-by-step guide to participation (contributor, independent node, satellite project).

- Provide templates, checklists, and community rules to reduce barriers to participation.

Important files:
- `INTRODUCTION.md` — Summary of philosophy and vision.
- `JOIN_GUIDE.md` — Step-by-step guide to join.
- `ROADMAP.md` — Milestones and priorities.
- `COMMUNITY_RULES.md` — Community code of conduct.
- `CONTRIBUTING.md` — Technical contribution process.
- `GOVERNANCE.md` — Lightweight governance structure (DAO-like concepts).

- `SECURITY.md` — Security guidelines, vulnerability reporting.

- `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/` and `.github/PULL_REQUEST_TEMPLATE.md`.
